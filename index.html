Introduction

My name is Gabriella Frenes. I'm a student journalist at University of California Berkeley. I major in media studies and have a minor in journalism. As a journalist I cover identity politics, culture, and social justice issues. I have a passion for audio storytelling and hope to work on a professional podcast one day.

I'm currently the Editor-in-Chief for an online publication, VOCES, and have done audio production for KALX News at UC Berkeley. After graduating I plan to continue my journalism work through freelancing and producing my own audio work.
